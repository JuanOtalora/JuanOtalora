INSIDE

Inside is a videogame experience enhanced using virtual 3d Sound. Our plan was to create a game that was able to change the perspective of visual aid. Meanwhile we are able to create real 3d sound enviroments.

INCAGE

Incage is a VR puzzle game that relies heavily in Virtual envieroments to create and solve simple puzzles. The idea is to create a space station where the player is trapped and needs to solve a series of puzzles to escape.

HISTORIAS DE UN CRONISTA

Historias de un Cronista is a project based on works with at-risk people located in the center of Bogot. We had to create an application to help workers at Plaza de Bolivar. We were helped by photographers who also worked as historians of the place.
